This website is a place I can maintain myself. It holds more context than a social-media profile and makes my engineering work easier to inspect.

## What I Will Publish

- Project logs and technical notes
- Learning reflections
- NUPACE exchange preparation and experience
- AI-assisted workflows and their limitations

Content is written in Markdown, reviewed, version controlled with Git, and deployed through GitHub Pages.
